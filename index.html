CMPE 255 Short Story

Tracking the Dynamics in Crowdfunding

Pranav Prem
011549707


What is crowdfunding?


Practise of funding a project by raising contributions from a large number of people


Money is taken from people in exchange for perks


These companies allow you to create campaigns with perks to attract funding.



Clearly, it helps knowing how much money the campaign is worth


in order to determine the worth of the perks.


In this short story, we see how to predict a daily funding amount for a campaign.

A hierarchical model is used
With input as
Funding sequence of a campaign and
Features of the campaign and perks
to estimate the
Amount of funding in the near future

Two-level time series: Campaign Level Forecasts (fc) Perk Level base forecasts (fp)
